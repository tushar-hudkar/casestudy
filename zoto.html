Vipul's

I think we need to be really careful with the payment screens. This time, we will also add Bank Accounts as the payment mode apart from Debit or Credit Cards.

Dekh lio, how would you work out in one screen. Also, what I feel is - the way paytm and others do their saved cards - user ko data nahi chalta ki card saved hai and here-enters the card details afresh. Their screens appear cluttered as well, to me.

and

the whole payment process needs to be fast.

1thing

Just remember, fastness comes with clarity & consistency -not with less number of clicks. Rest has been well taken care of.
We have added one extra click to the whole payment process (in comparison to paytm or flipkart or others) so to allow for the following -

  • Consistent experience the first time & the next.
  • Pick up saved cards with ease & clarity.
  • Keep enough blank space, still.
